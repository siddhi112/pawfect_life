Hello USER, I, Dr. Ross Jack Geller, founder of 'All Are Pawfect', would love to guide you all through our website. This website is built for those genuine Animal Lovers, who would go to any extent to help, save or give a healthy life for an animal without hesitating.
In this website, we provide you with
  • An emergency number--> If you see an animal not well/being abused.
  • Adoption Facility--> We put up the photos and details of the pets ready to be adopted after doing all the necessary vaccination and treatment for the well being of the animal. However, a date will be fixed for an interview and a "home environment for pets" check will be conducted by us to verify whether the pet will be have a healthy lifestyle or not.
  • Join Our Team-->We request you to consider joining our team only if you are really passionate about animals and are willing to help whenever needed. You will be asked for your contact details and an interview will be conducted by us.
    • NOTE: You should strictly be above 18+ years of age to apply for an interview
  • Donation Facility--> Donations for the well being of our animals is accepted from anyone willing to give any amount of money that the donor desires.
For any additional queries, contact the numbers provided at the bottom of the page.
